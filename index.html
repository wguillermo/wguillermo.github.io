About Me

I am a full stack web developer, college grad, and military veteran. The programming languages I use in developing programs are as follows:

  • Front-End: JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS, AJAX, Bootstrap, CSS3, HTML5
  • Middle Tier: C#, ASP.NET MVC, .NET 4.5, Web API, ADO.NET
  • Back-End: T-SQL, SQL Server 2014
  • Other: Team Foundation Server, Visual Studio 2015, Agile/Scrum Methodologies, Trello

I served as an Airborne Infantryman (aka Paratrooper) with the elite 82nd Airborne, having completed two combat deployments to Iraq. Upon my discharge from military service, I moved back to California and ventured off in pursuit of my college degree. As a first generation college grad (go Bruins!) it makes me proud to have reached this goal. Now I am in pursuit of developing software for the entertainment and/or benefit of people.
