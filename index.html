About Me

I am a combat veteran, having completed two deployments to Iraq. Upon completion of my military service, I moved back to California and ventured off in pursuit of my college degree, while working fulltime. I'm a first generation college grad, and now I am a full stack web developer (no, I'm not Spiderman). The following are the languages and skills that I use and implement in developing programs:

  • Front-End: JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS, AJAX, Bootstrap, CSS3, HTML5
  • Middle Tier: C#, ASP.NET MVC, .NET 4.5, Web API, ADO.NET
  • Back-End: T-SQL, SQL Server 2014
  • Other: Team Foundation Server, Visual Studio 2015, Agile/Scrum Methodologies, Trello

Project Description

Lash Girl Club

Lash Girl Club is a complete website developed by myself and 12 other developers for an entreprenuer who wanted to expand her beauty business by creating a subscription-based box of beauty and health products to include eye lash extensions. I developed the blogs portion of the site - developed the front-end, middle tier, and back-end. I had also work on login redirects and another feature that was eventually deprecated. I had also refactored codes from jQuery to AngularJS.

Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock

Everyone loves a good ol' game of rock-paper-scissors, right? Well, how about rock-paper-scissors-lizard-spock? This is a variation of the classic game developed by Sam Kass and Karen Bryla, and popularized by the character Sheldon Cooper of the hit comedy The Big Bang Theory. I programmed this game to include lizard and Spock, and implemented counters for the round and scores using JavaScript and jQuery.
